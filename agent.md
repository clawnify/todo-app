# Todo App (Issue Tracker)

A Linear-style issue tracker with projects, labels, and comments.

## What this app does

- Track issues/tasks with status (backlog, todo, in progress, done, cancelled), priority, labels, and due dates
- Organize issues into projects with progress tracking
- Add colored labels to categorize issues
- Comment on issues for collaboration
- Auto-generated issue identifiers (TASK-1, TASK-2, etc.)
